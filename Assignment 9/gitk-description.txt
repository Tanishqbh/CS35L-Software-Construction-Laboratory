gitk Description
Upper Left Window:
	-shows the series of commits to the repository, with the most recent on top
	-it also shows the commit message and the branches that were modified
	
Upper Middle Window:
	-shows the users that applied the corresponding changes

Uper Right Window:
	-shows the date of the change

Lower Left Window:
	-shows the commit details such as author, committer, parent, child, branches etc...
	-it also contains the changes in diff format

Lower Right Window:
	-shows the files that were changed or modified by the particular commit
